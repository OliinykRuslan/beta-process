Our recruitment process

Getting to know you

We want to know everything about you. To accomplish this we schedule a online video chat or a meeting at one of our offices, what ever you prefer.

Before the meeting

To have a productive meeting we need some extra information. It shouldn’t take more then 10 minutes. We need this information to make a seamless match between you and our partner companies. We will be needing information about your salary wish, ideal jobs. Furthermore you can make a small test which will help us validating your personality traits and learning ability.

2
3

Meeting time

This the moment to shine: tell us about what you want in your new job. We will discuss the outcomes of all the information you gave to us before the meeting.

Partner introductions

During the meeting we will discuss all the possible job options at our partner companies. You are always in charge where you want to be introduced. Betaselect does nothing without your permission. You can have real time insight in the recruitment process in your online portal

4
5

Job interviews

When the partner company is just as enthusiastic about the match as you and we are you will be invited for a job interview. This could be online or at the company location. We are here to help you during the process. We will always schedule a interview prep in which we tell you everything we know about the partner company, interview panel and the recruitment process.

Job offer

If the interviews are positive, you will receive one or more job offers, congratulations! See us as your sidekick, we can negotiate on your behalf.

6
7

Working at Betaselect

During the secondment we are your employer, we are there to help you find your way in the company and support you. We schedule regular meetings during the secondment between you and Betaselect. Near the end of the secondment we reach out the you regarding the transition to the partnercompany.
